Our Story

Where Fire Meets Passion

Fuoco was born from a simple dream — to bring the authentic taste of Naples to the world. Our founder, Marco Rossi, grew up in a small coastal town outside Naples, watching his grandmother knead dough every Sunday morning. The aroma of fresh basil, the crackle of the wood-fired oven, the joy of family gathered around a table — these were the moments that shaped his soul.

In 2009, Marco left Italy with nothing but his grandmother's recipe book and a burning passion. He arrived in New York with a mission: to create pizza that doesn't just feed people — it tells a story. He found a tiny storefront in Manhattan, built a brick oven by hand, and opened Fuoco — Italian for "fire" — named after the flame that transforms simple ingredients into something extraordinary.

Those early days were hard. Marco slept in the kitchen, worked 18-hour days, and served just a handful of customers. But word spread quickly. People tasted the difference — the 72-hour fermented dough, the San Marzano tomatoes imported from Italy, the fresh mozzarella made daily. Within a year, lines stretched around the block.

— Marco Rossi, Founder

Our Journey

The Fuoco Timeline

From a dream in Naples to becoming New York's most beloved pizzeria — every milestone tells our story.

2009

The First Spark

Marco Rossi opens the first Fuoco in a tiny Manhattan storefront, hand-building the brick oven himself.

2011

First Award

Fuoco wins "Best New Pizzeria" from New York Food Magazine. The team grows from 3 to 15 members.

2013

Second Location

After overwhelming demand, Fuoco opens its second location in Brooklyn with an open kitchen.

2015

Delivery Launch

Fuoco launches delivery with a custom heat-retaining box. "30 minutes or free" becomes iconic.

2017

100K Customers

Fuoco serves its 100,000th customer. NYC Mayor declares "Fuoco Pizza Day" on March 15th.

2019

10 Year Anniversary

A decade of fire! Fuoco celebrates with a street festival and opens location #3 in Queens.

2021

Innovation & Resilience

Through the pandemic, Fuoco pivots to contactless "Oven-to-Door" and launches gluten-free options.

2024

The Fuoco Family Today

5 locations, 120+ varieties, 50,000+ happy customers, 35+ awards. Marco still makes dough every morning.
